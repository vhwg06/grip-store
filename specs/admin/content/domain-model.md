# Content Domain Model

## Core Objects

- `MediaAsset`
- `BannerSet`
- `Article`
- `FAQEntry`
- `AboutNarrative`
- `ProductEditorialContent`

## State Concepts

- media `available` vs `in use`
- article `draft` vs `published`
- FAQ `active` vs `inactive`
- banner `active` vs `inactive`

## Derived Meanings

- a published article becomes public editorial knowledge
- an active banner becomes page representation
- an in-use asset becomes protected content infrastructure
